DogeDealz LLC "DogeDealz" is a Wyoming Based Limited Liability Company with headquarters at 30 N Gould Street Suite R in Sheridan, WY 82801 USA.

DogeDealz is committed to being a compliance first organization that understands the need for common sense regulation in the crypto-currency space.

Some early examples of our commitment to make every reasonable effort to be compliant with applicable rules and regulations given the heretofore lack of regulatory clarity are:

  • Registration with FinCEN as a Money Service Business
  • Establishment of an Anti-Money-Laundering (AML) policy
  • Willingness to perform payment industry standard independent PCI audits and reviews of our technology
  • Commitment to not participate in any "CVC Mixing" activities as defined by FinCEN
  • Non-custodial implementation of the GigaWallet dogecoin payment service
    • DogeDealz is crypto-currency wallet agnostic
    • Payouts for e-commerce orders are released to third-party merchant wallets at the time of transaction
